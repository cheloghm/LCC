About Us

William Marrion Branham

William Marrion Branham (1909–1965) was a devoted servant of God, recognized as a prophet who delivered the End Time Message. His ministry was distinguished by divine healings, miracles, and profound biblical teachings that illuminated the mysteries of the Scriptures.

Branham’s mission was to restore believers to the apostolic faith and prepare the Bride of Christ for His imminent return. His teachings emphasize the purity of Christ and the necessity of adhering strictly to the Word of God.
